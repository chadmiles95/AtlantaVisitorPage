Visit Atlanta

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district close to the center of Atlanta.

Top three activities to do in Atlanta

Go to the local park

Piedmont Park is a great park for a picnic or just to walk around and take in nature.

Attend a show or concert

The Fox Theatre is an award winning theatre with some of the best concerts in Georgia.

Watch a Falcons Game

The Mercedes Benz Stadium is a beautiful stadium for watching a falcons game.
